Document 1: Product Requirements Document (PRD)
1. Introduction

This document outlines the product requirements for a new freelance platform designed to connect companies with skilled freelance professionals. The platform will facilitate the entire hiring process, from talent discovery and communication to interview scheduling and mission management. Its primary goal is to create an efficient and centralized marketplace for the freelance economy.

2. User Personas

Freelancer (e.g., Alex): A skilled professional looking for flexible work opportunities. Alex wants to easily showcase their skills, set their rates, and find missions that match their expertise and availability.

Company Representative (e.g., Sarah): A hiring manager or project lead who needs to find and hire talent for specific projects quickly. Sarah needs a reliable way to search for qualified candidates, review their credentials, and manage the hiring process.

Platform Administrator: An internal user responsible for maintaining the health and integrity of the platform, managing users, and curating professional categories and skills.

3. Product Goals

To provide companies with a fast and efficient way to find and hire qualified freelancers.

To offer freelancers a platform to find relevant job opportunities and manage their careers.

To streamline communication and administrative tasks for both parties.

To ensure a high-quality user base through profile validation and a rating system.

4. Features & User Stories
Epic: User Onboarding & Authentication

As a user, I want to sign up with an email and password so I can access the platform.

As a user, I want to log in to my account securely and log out when I'm done.

As a freelancer, I must complete a mandatory three-step onboarding process to build my profile.

As a freelancer, I want to provide my personal information (phone, nationality, daily rate, operating cities) in the first step.

As a freelancer, I want to select my skills, industry, and categories in the second step.

As a freelancer, I want to upload my CV or skip this step during the third step of onboarding.

As a company, I must complete a simple onboarding process by providing my name, company name, and industry.

Epic: Company Experience

As a company, I want to see a list of freelancers and be able to filter them by skills, category, availability, daily rate, and location.

As a company, I want to view a freelancer's detailed profile, including their CV, to assess their suitability.

As a company, I want to send messages to freelancers to start a conversation.

As a company, I want to schedule interviews with freelancers and manage them (view today's, upcoming, and past interviews). I also want to be able to reschedule or cancel interviews.

As a company, I want to set my own availability for interviews.

As a company, I want to create and send official job propositions (missions) to freelancers.

As a company, I want to view and manage all my ongoing and finished missions.

As a company, I want to rate a freelancer after a mission is completed.

As a company, I want to edit my profile, view policies, and delete my account.

Epic: Freelancer Experience

As a freelancer, I want a dashboard to find job offers, with filters for keywords, activities, and location.

As a freelancer, I want to view the details of a job offer and message the company.

As a freelancer, I want to set a status to indicate if I am available for new missions.

As a freelancer, I want to manage my missions and mark them as finished.

As a freelancer, I want to rate a company after a mission is completed.

As a freelancer, I want to manage my interview schedule.

As a freelancer, I want to edit my profile information, including my CV and daily rate, and delete my account.

Epic: Back Office Administration

As an admin, I want full CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) control over freelancer and company profiles, including the ability to approve pending registrations.

As an admin, I want full CRUD control over the platform's taxonomy (skills, categories, and industries).

As an admin, I want to view all signed contracts (collaborations) on the platform.

As an admin, I want to manage interviews between users if necessary.

As an admin, I want full CRUD control over the list of "Portage Companies" and the ability to assign them to freelancers.
